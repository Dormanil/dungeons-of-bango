# Status Effects

Conditions that can affect the player or the enemies. 
#### Attack Down

Lowers strength by 25%. May Stack.
#### Bleeding

Inflicts damage equal to 5% of the afflicted's  max HP points each turn.
#### Blinded

Makes the whole map black. The player can only see the player unit and use menus.
#### Confused

It causes the afflicted's movement or attack hit in a random direction.
#### Cursed

Makes the  user unable to attack for a set amount of turns
#### Defense Down

Lowers the  afflicted's defense by 25%. May Stack
#### Disarmed

Makes the afflicted drop their weapon to an adjacent tile to where they're standing.
#### Frozen

Makes the afflicted unable to move. An attack on the afflicted or an item may undo the condition.
#### Burning

Inflicts damage equal to 5% of the afflicted's max HP points each turn. Also lowers defense by 25%
#### Paralyzed

Makes the afflicted unable to move. May attack or use items.
#### Poisoned

Inflicts damage equal to 5% of the afflicted's max HP points each turn. Also lowers attack by 25%
#### Silenced

Makes the afflicted unable to use magic.
#### Sleeping

Makes the whole map black. Makes the player unable to move or act for a set amount of turns.
#### Slow

Slows down the player movement by 1.

Berserk

Makes the afflicted attack the closest unit to it.
