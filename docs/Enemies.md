# Enemies

Enemies are the units that are gonna opposite to the player. For the most part they will  attack on sight. 

### Types of Enemies (WIP)

- Normal enemies 
- Strong Enemies
- FOE (Fantastically Overtuned enemies)
- Boss

#### Normal Enemies

![[Normal Skeleton.png]]

Normal Enemies only have a single weapon a armor.  No accessories or rare equipment.
#### Strong Enemies

![[Scythe Dude.png]]

Strong enemies will have rare weapons with effects and accessories. They're a good way to stock up on good equipment
#### FOE

![[Spoder.png|195]]

Special Enemies that will wander the labyrinth in a set  pattern, only deviating from it if they spot the player. They too strong to face at first sight and have the best weapons and items available in the labyrinth. (If we can't  make them bigger than normal enemies, maybe some sort of evil glow to denote they're very bad?)
#### Boss

![[Medusa thing.png|190]]

These enemies are the final challenge of their labyrinth areas and will guard the way to the next area of the labyrinths. Their floor will probably necessitate a floor with some kind of puzzle to make them easier to defeat.
