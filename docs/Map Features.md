### Slippery Tile

When the player steps on this tile, they will continue moving every turn, and won't be able to change their direction until they hit an obstacle or a regular tile.
### Trap Tile

Trap Tiles have various effects that are triggered automatically when the player steps on them.  A trap tile can poison the player, hurt them, teleport them, slows them down. (We can add more later on)

### Unlockable Shortcut Tiles

Tiles that allow you to teleport through one point to another. They can only be unlocked from one direction, but once unlocked can be used from both sides.
